Our Services

Pet Drop-In Visits

We offer professional pet sitting and drop-in services to ensure your pet feels loved and cared for when you can't be there. Our services include feeding, walking, medication administration, and plenty of playtime.

Animal Nutrition Consulting

Our certified animal nutritionists provide expert guidance on dietary needs specific to your pet. We create customized nutrition plans to address health concerns, allergies, weight management, and overall wellness.

Emergency Pet Care

Need last-minute pet care? We've got you covered. Our flexible emergency pet care services ensure your furry friends are never left alone, even when unexpected situations arise.

About Us

Cyd's Creature Care was founded by Ria with a simple mission: to provide loving, professional care for pets while offering expert nutrition guidance to pet owners. As a certified animal care specialist and nutritionist, Ria is passionate about animal health and wellbeing.

Ria believes that proper nutrition and regular care are the foundations of a happy, healthy pet. Her services are designed to support busy pet owners who want the very best for their furry family members.

Whether you need someone to check in on your pets while you're at work, or you're looking for specialized nutrition advice, Ria is here to help with compassion and expertise.
